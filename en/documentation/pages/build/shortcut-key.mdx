## Pro Tips

<Tip>
- Combine shortcuts for faster workflows (e.g., `Ctrl+D` then `Shift` to duplicate and inspect dependencies)
- Use `Ctrl/Cmd + 1` frequently to keep your entire workflow visible
- The hand tool (`H`) is perfect for navigating complex workflows with many nodes
</Tip>
